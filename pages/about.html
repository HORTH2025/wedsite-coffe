Who We Are

Coffee Shop in Cambodia

Coffee Shop is a natural coffee brand located in Mondulkiri Province, Cambodia. It focuses on high-quality coffee beans that are cultivated by local Cambodian farmers, following standard agricultural practices. The coffee is hand-picked to ensure only the best beans are selected, contributing to its rich flavor profile.
